# Matrix Observation

Several themes converge repeatedly around four governance questions:

1. Who retains authority?
2. What makes execution admissible?
3. How is governance observed?
4. What evidence demonstrates governance effectiveness?

These recurring questions form the conceptual center of the portfolio.
