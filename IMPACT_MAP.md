# Impact Map v1.0

## Purpose

This document maps portfolio artifacts to the governance problems they address, the insights they produced, and the outcomes they demonstrate.

The objective is not impact measurement.

The objective is governance traceability.

---

# Artifact: IPC Research Series

## Problem

Many governance discussions focus on capability while governance conditions remain implicit.

## Insight

Governance concepts become stronger when explored through iterative public research and external discussion.

## Implementation

IPC1 → IPC9

Public governance research chain.

## Outcome

A documented evolution of governance concepts including:

* authority
* admissibility
* continuity
* evidence
* governance observability

---

# Artifact: Autonomy Readiness Checker (ARC)

## Problem

Capability assessments often ignore governance readiness.

## Insight

Governance evaluation requires explicit separation between extraction, evaluation and authority.

## Implementation

Deterministic governance gate.

Rule-based evaluation system.

## Outcome

Executable demonstration that:

* evaluation is bounded
* authority remains external
* governance conditions can be assessed deterministically

# Artifact: Controlled Email Intake Engine (CEIE)

## Problem

Governance is often declared but difficult to observe during runtime.

## Insight

Governance quality becomes visible through evidence, replayability and runtime observation.

## Implementation

Governance architecture repository with:

* pressure program
* runtime evidence review
* investigation phase
* semantic governance architecture

## Outcome

Demonstrates:

* governance observability
* replay verification
* evidence-first evolution
* audit-oriented governance design
